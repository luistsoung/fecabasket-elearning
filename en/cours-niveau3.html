Level 5Level 3 · WABC Level 3 — Elite

The peak of the FECABASKET curriculum. For head coaches of elite teams, future regional DTNs and trainers of trainers. Annual periodization, leadership, staff management, coach training and personal dissertation.

FormatCapsules + MCQs + Quizzes + Dissertation
AudienceHead coach · Trainer · Regional DTN
PrerequisitesLevel 2 completed

Level overview

Level 3 (equivalent to WABC Level 3) certifies the coach able to lead a complete sport program across an annual season. They drive a multidisciplinary staff, plan via periodization, manage an elite roster, and in turn train other coaches. Validation goes through a personal dissertation of 30-50 pages defended in front of a jury.

  • Plan a complete annual season with periodization
  • Lead an elite team (recruitment, roster management, leadership)
  • Drive a multidisciplinary staff (S&C, physio, statistician, video)
  • Run a sport project with KPIs and institutional reporting
  • Train other coaches (knowledge transfer, mentoring)
  • Carry out an applied research project (dissertation)
  • Represent the club/federation to media and institutions

1

Annual periodization and planning

⏱ 14 hours

Objectives

  • Build a macro-periodization (pre-season, regular season, playoffs, off-season)
  • Break down into meso-cycles (4-6 weeks) and micro-cycles (week)
  • Adjust training loads (volume, intensity, technique, tactics)
  • Integrate FIBA windows (national teams, breaks)

Detailed outline

1.1 · Macro-cyclePre-season (6 weeks) · season · playoffs · off · transition
1.2 · Meso-cycle4-6 weeks · 1 dominant theme
1.3 · Micro-cycleTypical week · 5-7 sessions · 1 game · recovery
1.4 · Load managementRPE · TQR · monotony · acute/chronic ratio
1.5 · FIBA windowsCompatibility with national team · absence management
1.6 · TaperingPre-playoff sharpening · volume reduction / intensity maintained
AssessmentQuiz + complete macro + micro submitted and orally defended (15 min). Reference videos available in French version.
