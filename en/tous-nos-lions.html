🎯 Purpose of the A' team

AfroCan is reserved for players competing on African soil (African clubs only). It is the showcase for local talent and the priority feeder to the senior national team. Next AfroCan edition: TBC by FIBA Africa.

🎯 How the qualification was earned

Thanks to their 2nd place (silver medal) at the 2025 U16 AfroBasket in Kigali, the young Lions earned the first U17 World Cup qualification in their history. First U16 AfroBasket debutant nation to directly qualify.
