🎓 Pedagogical principles for using these drills

  • Respect the level: never run a Level 3 play with U12 players. Start with the right tier.
  • Decompose: each play in 3-4 steps. Run them slowly, then accelerate.
  • Repeat: 6-8 repetitions per play before adding defense.
  • Adapt: don't hesitate to simplify (fewer options) or modify based on your roster.
  • Connect to the manual: each play has an equivalent in the FIBA WABC manual. Read the corresponding chapter.
