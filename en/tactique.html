Click any tile to open the step-by-step animation of the drill or play on thehoopsgeek.com. Each level offers a selection tailored to pedagogical complexity: Mini-Basket (basic drills), Monitor (first systems), Levels 1-2-3 (increasingly advanced sets up to NBA/EuroLeague schemes).
