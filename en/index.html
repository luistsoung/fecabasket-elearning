Why FECABASKET E-Learning? 🇫🇷 FR · 🇬🇧 EN

Designed for the Cameroonian context: mobile-first, low-data friendly, content in French and English, taught by the country's FIBA WABC instructors.

01

Mobile-first

29 million mobile connections in Cameroon. 5-10 min video capsules are optimized for smartphones and lighter networks.

02

Animated tactics

Interactive diagrams and animated plays inspired by FIBA WABC, FastModel and Hoop Coach. Truly understand what you read on the whiteboard.

03

Quizzes & assessments per module

Assess your knowledge. 70% minimum to validate. Instant pedagogical feedback on every question, with the option to retry.

04

FIBA WABC-equivalent content

Aligned with international standards: our modules faithfully reflect the FIBA WABC (World Association of Basketball Coaches) reference, ensuring skills directly comparable to coaches trained abroad.

05

Efficient preparation for federal diplomas

A true launchpad to the official FECABASKET diplomas (Animator, Monitor, Levels 1-2-3): targeted capsules, self-assessment quizzes and case studies aligned with the federal exams. Arrive prepared, validate with confidence.

DTN Roadmap — 2026/2027 season

From Q3 2026 to 2028: a controlled rollout

Five successive phases to scale from 50 coaches trained per year to 500+ certified nationwide.

Phase 1 · Q3 2026

Initialization

Moodle platform setup, FECABASKET branding, VPS infrastructure, hosting RFP. Launch of the elearning.fecabasket.com subdomain in July-September 2026.

Phase 2 · Q4 2026

Educational production

Filming and producing video capsules for Animator + Monitor + Level 1. Integration of Campay payment (Orange Money + MTN MoMo).

Phase 3 · Q1-Q2 2027

Pilot — 100 coaches

Launch in 2 leagues: Centre and Littoral. Field feedback, pedagogical adjustments, validation by FIBA experts.

Phase 4 · Q3-Q4 2027

Nationwide rollout

Expansion to the 10 regions. Production of Levels 2 and 3. Target: 500 certified coaches by end of 2028.

Phase 5 · 2028+

Consolidation & expansion

Three-year recertification, diaspora and francophone CEMAC zone outreach, FIBA WABC partnerships.
