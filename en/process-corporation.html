Process Corporation

This YouTube channel caught our attention with its pragmatic and pedagogical approach. The FECABASKET DTN recommends it to coaches. (All Process Corp videos are in French — they are the original source.)

💬

Their motto

"The more you share, the more you learn — that's the miracle."

🇨🇲 Why it's valuable for Cameroonian coaches

🆓
Free on YouTube

Instant access to tactical analyses without subscription. Ideal for coaches in rural areas or with limited budgets.

📐
Tactical depth

10-15 min analyses breaking down pro systems (Paris Basket, French national team, NBA, EuroLeague). Rare expertise level.

🎓
Pedagogy at the core

Clear animated diagrams, accessible vocabulary. Built to grow coaches, not to show off.
